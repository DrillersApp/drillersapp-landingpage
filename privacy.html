This Privacy Policy explains how Drillers ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). The App is a fitness/workout application and does not use cameras, microphones, or connect to external health devices by default.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to keep data collection to a minimum. Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and a username.
  • Workout & App Data: Workouts you create or complete, goals, progress, and in-app preferences (e.g. saved routines, custom notes).
  • Technical & Usage Data: Device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and analytics about how you use the App (to help us improve performance).

We do not collect: health data from external devices (for example, Apple Health, Garmin, Fitbit), camera or microphone data, or location data — unless you explicitly enable a feature that requests it. If the App integrates with third-party health platforms in the future, we will update this policy and obtain any required consents.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App's features (save workouts, sync user progress).
  • Improve the App and fix bugs (analytics and crash reports).
  • Send transactional messages, such as account or billing notices, if applicable.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests for information.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We will not sell your personal information. We may disclose information to:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors that provide infrastructure, analytics, crash reporting, or other services (for example, cloud hosting or analytics). These providers are contractually restricted from using your information for other purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to respond to legal process or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

4. Third-Party Services & Links

The App may use third-party services for analytics (e.g., Firebase, Amplitude, or similar). These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. The App may also contain links to third-party websites; we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no security system is impenetrable. Please treat account credentials and personal data with care.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes, such as analytics and legal compliance. If you request deletion of your account or personal data, we will delete it subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, data portability, and objection to certain processing. To exercise your rights, contact us at hailey@drillers.app. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Children

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

9. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, such as the right to know categories of personal information collected, and to request deletion or opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information). To submit a request, contact us at hailey@drillers.app.

10. European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, your data protection rights may include access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection. Our lawful bases for processing typically include performance of a contract, legitimate interests, and user consent where required.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will post the updated policy in the App and update the Effective Date above. Significant changes will be highlighted in the App or by email where feasible.
